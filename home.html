A Forum for LIFE, Inc. is a 501 (c) 3 New York & California based non-profit corporation with Public Charity status.

Mission Statement

A Forum for LIFE, Inc. was established to promote the health, safety and well-being of all people by presenting education and training programs designed to assist participants in helping others in need.


In Community Partnership with organizations such as; the New York City Department of Health & Mental Hygiene, the NYC Board of Education, the Department of Youth & Community Development, the Department of Homeless Services and Early Learn NYC we offer vital financial support for Professional Development.

As an Authorized Provider for the Emergency Care & Safety Institute, A Forum for LIFE provides all levels emergency response training such as CPR with A.ED. & First Aid certification. Community based organizations, schools, daycare centers, individuals of low to moderate income, women in need, and at risk youth are among those who benefit from our mission.


Offered Programs

Key facets that foster self-actualization include family stability, solid academic background, career goals, healthy lifestyle and mental hygiene. Participants gain strong core values and solid foundations for productive living through these programs:

  • Academic Tutoring & Mentoring Programs
  • Adult CPR with A.E.D. & Advanced First Aid
  • CPR for the Professional Rescuer with A.E.D.
  • Pediatric & Adult CPR with A.E.D.
  • Culinary Arts & Nutrition Classes
  • Early Childhood Development Programs
  • Fatherhood Initiatives
  • Financial Planning
  • Heart Health - Lowering High Blood Pressure
  • Lifeguard Certification & Water Safety Training
  • Literacy
  • Medication Administration Training
  • Pediatric CPR
  • Sports & Recreation Programs / Golf Lessons
  • Stress Management
